Pre-Game:
17 year-old Onuris lives with his parents, preparing to head to college. He lives in Egypt in the 1940s. 

His parents were killed in a bombing in 1941, and he joined the army to defeat those who killed his parents. The war ends and he still feels unsatisfied. He was able to defeat his enemies, but his parents were still gone. 


He researches the history of Egypt and the gods in order to see where his parents might have gone. In his search, he comes across a group of truthers, a cult. They promise him to deliver him to the Underworld through a ritual. In the ritual, he is killed and enters the Underworld.


In order to get his parents back, Onuris must fight enemies from the past, present, and future. 



ENDING:
Onuris defeats Osiris after a long and grueling battle. Once defeated, Onuris is required to stay and rule the Underworld. With his newfound power, however, he is able to bring his parents back to life, sending them back to their old house. FIN.
